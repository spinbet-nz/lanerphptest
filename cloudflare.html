Speed

How a content delivery network works

A CDN is a network of servers spread across the globe. Instead of every visitor fetching your site from one origin server, the CDN keeps cached copies in data centers close to users. When someone visits, the nearest "edge" server responds — cutting latency dramatically.

Cloudflare operates one of the largest networks on earth, with data centers in hundreds of cities. Static assets — images, CSS, scripts — are served from the edge, so pages load faster and your origin server handles far less traffic.

Infrastructure

DNS & SSL

Cloudflare runs one of the fastest public DNS networks in the world, including the well-known 1.1.1.1 resolver. When you use Cloudflare for your site, your DNS records are managed from a single dashboard and resolved at the edge.

It also provides free SSL/TLS certificates, so traffic between visitors and the edge is encrypted (the padlock in the browser) without you manually buying or installing a certificate.
