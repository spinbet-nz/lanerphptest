Who we are

Making the web understandable

StackGuide is an independent educational publisher. Since 2019 we've helped curious readers and small business owners understand the technology that powers the modern internet.

Our mission

Clarity over hype

The web is full of marketing claims and acronyms. Our job is to cut through that noise with accurate, vendor-neutral explanations — so you can understand your options and make confident decisions.

We cover the services people actually use: Google's ecosystem, domain registration, content delivery networks, security, and more. Every article is written to be genuinely useful to a real person, not to sell you something.
